Welcome to Wine Tour

Wine Tours

Situated between Lake Ontario and Lake Erie, the Niagara Peninsula offers the perfect climate for wine production. The wineries of Niagara on the Lake and The Niagara Escarpment have gained worldwide acclaim. Located just a short drive from Niagara Falls, Niagara on the Lake, “The Prettiest Town in Canada”, is steeped in history and has over 35 wineries within its borders. The visitor experience found in Niagara on the Lake is totally unique. Here, wine and cuisine go hand-in-hand with history.
