Canada`s Wonderland

It’s really no wonder that Canada’s Wonderland is one of the country’s premier amusement parks. The place has 18 bona fide roller coasters—third-most in the world—and more than 200 attractions with a 20-acre water park. Located in Vaughan, conveniently close to Toronto, this thrilling amusement park is the perfect place to spend a hot summer day or a relaxing fall afternoon. With mind-boggling coasters, joyful rides for kids and tons of games to play, there’s fun for the whole family at Canada’s Wonderland. The attached waterpark is perfect for toasty afternoons, and it’s an attraction in its own right. But it’s after dark that Wonderland really shines, with fireworks displays lighting up over Wonder Mountain.
