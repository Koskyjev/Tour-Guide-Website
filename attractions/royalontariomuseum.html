Royal Ontario Museum

Is there life on Mars? Who knows! What we do know is that there are meteorites at the ROM, three of which are Martian. Meteorites (or space rocks) are surprisingly common, with over 100 having landed on Earth to date, some even originating from the moon. Find them at the ROM’s Earth’s Treasures department during your next visit or browse the ROM’s online database.
