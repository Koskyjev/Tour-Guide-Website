Art Gallery of Toronto

The Art Gallery of Ontario owns one of Toronto’s most popular pieces Before Yayoi Kusama’s Let’s Survive Forever enchanted the city, one of the AGO’s most popular acquisitions was Henry Moore’s Large Two Forms. In fact, it’s considered one of Toronto’s most photographed works of art. The avant-garde, corporeal structure is the largest of Henry Moore’s sculptures displayed at the museum. Find it at the AGO’s rear, in Grange Park.
